Welcome to ACISP 2026

The 31st Australasian Conference on Information Security and Privacy (ACISP 2026) will be held in Perth, Australia on 6-9 July 2026. It will be organized by the University of Western Australia (UWA) and Edith Cowan University (ECU). The proceeding will be published by Springer as a volume of the Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series.
