Important Notes:

  • Each accepted paper must be covered by an Author Registration.
  • Author Registration deadlines:
    • Round 1: 6 February 2026.
    • Round 2: 8 May 2026.
  • Program Committee (PC) Members may register using the discounted PC Member rate shown above.
  • Student Registration is available to currently enrolled students; a valid student ID will be checked at on-site registration.
  • For registration support, email jin.hong@uwa.edu.au with subject “ACISP Registration”.
  • Registration fees are non-refundable (registrations cannot be cancelled)
  • Registration transfers may be considered with a valid reason. Email jin.hong@uwa.edu.au with subject “ACISP Transfer Registration” and include a short explanation. Note: Student Registration cannot be transferred to non-students.
  • If you require an invoice, email jin.hong@uwa.edu.au with subject “ACISP Registration Invoice”.

Invitation Letter Request:

If you require an official invitation letter, please email jin.hong@uwa.edu.au with subject "ACISP Invitation Letter Request" with the following details:

  • Full name (as per your passport)
  • Date of birth
  • Nationality
  • Affiliation
  • Mailing address
  • Phone number (with country/area code)
  • Paper title
